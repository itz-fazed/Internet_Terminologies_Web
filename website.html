Websites

A website is a collection of interlinked pages designed to deliver information to a wide audience[cite: 190, 193].

Its primary role is presentation, meaning the content should remain mostly static regardless of who is viewing it[cite: 194, 195].

  • Key Examples: A company's landing page or a digital portfolio[cite: 196].
  • Focus: Informational content rather than complex user-driven logic[cite: 193].
