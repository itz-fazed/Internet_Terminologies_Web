Website

A website is a collection of interlinked pages designed to deliver information to a wide audience.

Its primary role is presentation, meaning the content should remain mostly static regardless of who is viewing it.

  • Key Examples: A company's landing page or a digital portfolio.
  • Focus: Informational content rather than complex user-driven logic.
