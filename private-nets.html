Intranet and Extranet

An Intranet is a private internal network used by a single organization to share information and resources securely among employees[cite: 104, 108].

An Extranet is a controlled extension of that private network that allows authorized outsiders, such as partners or vendors, to access specific parts of the data[cite: 106, 109, 110, 111].

Their primary role is to provide layers of security for collaboration both within and just outside a company[cite: 112, 113, 115, 116].
