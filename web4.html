Web 4.0: The Intelligent/Ubiquitous Web

Web 4.0 is the future of the web, involving seamless AI integration and the Internet of Things (IoT). It is meant to provide real-time, autonomous interaction between humans and machines.

Often called the "Intelligent Web," it makes the online experience ubiquitous, where information and connectivity are available everywhere and at all times through highly personalized and proactive systems.
